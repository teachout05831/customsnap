FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SiteForge.

How does the AI website builder work?

Simply describe your business in a few sentences - what you do, who you serve, and your style preferences. Our AI analyzes thousands of successful websites in your industry to create a custom design with professional copy, images, and layout tailored to your needs.

Do I need any technical skills?

Absolutely not! SiteForge is designed for non-technical users. If you can type a few sentences about your business, you can create a professional website. Our visual editor makes customization simple with drag-and-drop.

Can I use my own domain?

Yes! On our Pro and Business plans, you can connect any custom domain you own. We also offer domain purchasing directly through SiteForge if you need a new one.

What if I want to cancel?

You can cancel anytime with no questions asked. Your data remains accessible and exportable. We don't believe in lock-ins - we want you to stay because you love the product.

How is this different from Wix or Squarespace?

Traditional website builders require you to make hundreds of design decisions and write all your own content. SiteForge's AI does the heavy lifting - generating professional designs and copy in seconds. You just review and tweak.
